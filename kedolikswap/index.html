DEX Aggregator

A DEX aggregator is a platform or tool that connects to multiple decentralized exchanges (DEXs) to provide users with the best available price for a particular trade by sourcing liquidity from several DEXs at once. Instead of manually checking each DEX for the best price, a DEX aggregator automatically finds the best rates for users, improving the overall trading experience.

When integrating DEX aggregator with a platform like KedolikSwap, users can get access to liquidity from multiple decentralized exchanges across the Solana ecosystem and beyond, further improving the trading process.
